# Creative Brief

> **Structured front door for every content run.** Produced by `content-strategist` (Step 1)
> from the clarifying-question answers, then read by `blog-writer`, `visual-strategist`,
> `infographic-art-director`, `image-content-agent`, and all social agents for consistent
> tone, message, and visual direction.
>
> Methodology adapted from
> [`microsoft/content-generation-solution-accelerator`](https://github.com/microsoft/content-generation-solution-accelerator)
> (Creative Brief Interpretation). Fill every field — downstream agents treat blank fields
> as missing requirements, not "anything goes."

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Topic** | _(the subject of this run)_ |
| **Status** | `draft` \| `approved` |
| **Author / persona voice** | First-person practitioner ("sharing my learnings working with customers") |

## 1. Overview
_One paragraph: what this content is and why it exists now._

## 2. Objectives
- _Primary objective (the one job this content must do)_
- _Secondary objectives_

## 3. Target audience
- **Primary persona:** _(role, seniority, pain points)_
- **Secondary personas:** _(from Dimension Analysis, if any)_

## 4. Key message
_The single sentence a reader should remember. Everything supports this._

## 5. Tone & style
- _Tutorial / opinion / field guide / comparison_
- _Voice guardrails: conversational, data-driven, no corporate/fundraising framing_

## 6. Deliverables
- _Blog (single / series N parts), LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Reddit, Reel, YouTube, Medium/Substack/LinkedIn Article_

## 7. Visual guidelines
> Drives `visual-strategist`, `infographic-art-director`, and `image-content-agent`.
- **Mood / aesthetic:** _(e.g., clean modern technical, high-contrast, editorial)_
- **Brand palette:** design tokens (ACCENT `#1f6feb`, ACCENT_2 `#0d9488`, ACCENT_3 `#7c3aed`)
- **Hero / illustrative imagery (AI-generated, if enabled):** _scene/subject description,
  composition, ~30% negative space, **no embedded text**, color fidelity to brand palette_
- **Deterministic visuals:** diagrams / infographics / exhibits remain programmatic
- **Reference images for grounding:** _(paths/URLs the vision step should describe)_

## 8. Call to action
_What the reader should do next (read Part 2, try the framework, follow, etc.)._

## 9. Constraints & guardrails
- _Compliance, claims that need citations, no-human-likeness / sensitive-scene flags_
- _Timeline / sequencing constraints_
