# The 120x Spread: A Developer's Guide to Model Selection (Visual Guide)

GitHub Copilot's cheapest model costs 0.25x. The most expensive: 30x. That is a 120x spread. Apple ML Research found reasoning models burn extra tokens on simple tasks with zero quality gain — the expensive model is not always better (as of May 2026 — subject to change).
